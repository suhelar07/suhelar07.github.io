ChatN

My App on Google Play

ChatN helps you review your previous notifications and saves message content received through notifications, allowing you to revisit it anytime.

You can also browse recently viewed statuses and save them directly to your photo gallery for later access.

In addition, you can enable temporary local copies of media from supported apps after files have been downloaded to your device, allowing you to access them for a limited time if they become unavailable later.

ChatN works entirely on your device using only notifications and locally stored files. It does not access your conversations directly or share your data with any third parties.
